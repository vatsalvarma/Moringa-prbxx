What’s Special About This Drink?

Moringa has long been known as a powerhouse leaf in traditional diets, valued for its dense nutritional profile. Often called a “super green,” it brings together a wide range of natural nutrients in one simple ingredient.

Shake & Gulp Moringa Shot transforms this traditional leaf into a convenient, easy-to-enjoy format. Balanced with ginger for warmth and honey for smoothness, it delivers strong nourishment in a small, refreshing shot. Since natural extracts settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Moringa Shot can be included in everyone’s wellness routine.

A natural way to feel energized and balanced.

Shake & Gulp Moringa Shot carries that legacy forward — pure, green nourishment in a modern, convenient form.
